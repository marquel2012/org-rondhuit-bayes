This package includes programs that implement Naive Bayes Classification,
described in the book "Algorithms of the Intelligent Web":

Algorithms of the Intelligent Web
written by HARALAMBOS MARMANIS and DMITRY BABENKO
May, 2009 ISBN: 1933988665
http://www.manning.com/marmanis/

These programs are rewritten from the original ones in terms of package names
and removing dependency of other project (e.g. Apache Lucene), no more than that.
They are distributed under LGPL.
